Valence 7 in the Low JR/AO Jumpers

Valence has competed in a variety of countries, arenas and divisions. He is fearless in the ring and will always do anything to keep the jumps up. Now that he is 18, he mainly competes in the Low Children Jumpers and the Low Equitation. Valence 7 always manages to ribbon in close to every division no matter how difficult the competition. He will teach his rider how to perform the best in the ring and dominate the any arena of his or her choosing!
