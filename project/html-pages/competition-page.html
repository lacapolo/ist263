Low JR/AO Jumpers

Valence has competed in a variety of countries, arenas and divisions. He is fearless in the ring and will always do anything to keep the jumps up. Now that he is 18, he mainly competes in the Low Children Jumpers and the Low Equitation. In his prime he competed in the Medium and Low Junior Ametures with Lauren Fischer. Once he was purchased by Lauren Capolongo he mainly did the Low Children's Jumpers to educate the rider on how to navigate the ring but soon enough they were jumping 1.15 and had plans to move up the the Low A/O again. The best thing about Valence is, he will teach his rider how to perform the best in the ring and dominate the any arena of his or her choosing! Despite the fact that he is the best teacher, he is also an incredible teammate and is game to listen and win as long as the rider tells him to do so!
